Bio

Fall Out Boy is an American rock band formed in Wilmette, Illinois, a suburb of Chicago, in 2001.

Members

  • Patrick Stump – lead vocals (2001–present); rhythm guitar, keyboards (2002–present)
  • Joe Trohman – lead guitar, backing vocals (2001–present); keyboards (2013–present)
  • Pete Wentz – bass, unclean vocals, backing vocals (2001–present)
  • Andy Hurley – drums, percussion (2003–present); backing vocals (2013–present)
